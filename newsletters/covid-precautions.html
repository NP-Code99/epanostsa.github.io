COVID-19 precautions

COVID-19

There are many measures you can take to prevent yourself from being exposed to COVID-19. Here is what you can do to protect yourself and your family. Always wear a mask in public. Make sure it is over your nose and secure under your chin. Make sure your mask fits well and you can still breath easily in it. Stay at least six feet away from others.

This is about 2 arm's lengths. Avoid crowds and other tight spaces. Wash your hands. You should wash your hands when you have been in public areas. In addition, if you need to touch your face for any reason, wash your hands first. Remember, you should follow all of these precautions. Wearing a mask alone isn’t as effective as wearing a mask plus staying away from crowds. If you follow these tips, you will significantly reduce your chance of getting COVID.
