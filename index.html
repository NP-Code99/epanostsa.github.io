Diagnosis
With appointments happening through video calls, it can be difficult to get an accurate assessment of your health. However, with Epanos, you can be just as sure of your health as before. During your appointments, your doctor can send a questionnaire that will help determine and illness you might have. Your doctor can walk you through any tests you can carry out yourself, and will identify if something can only be determined by an in-person visit. Diagnosis can still be effective online.
